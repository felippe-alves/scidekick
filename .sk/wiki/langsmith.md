# LangSmith

LLM application tracing, evaluation, and observability platform from LangChain.

- **Site**: https://www.langchain.com/langsmith
- **Key features**: Trace visualization, evaluation runs, dataset management, human annotation

## Relevance to Scidekick

Referenced in the science surface as a system to integrate with. Scidekick should link to LangSmith traces rather than duplicate its tracing capabilities. OpenTelemetry import/export should support LangSmith traces where possible.
