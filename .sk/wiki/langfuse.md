# Langfuse

Open-source LLM engineering platform for traces, evals, prompt management, and metrics.

- **Site**: https://langfuse.com/
- **Key features**: Trace ingestion, evaluation, prompt versioning, cost tracking

## Relevance to Scidekick

Referenced in the science surface as a system to integrate with. Scidekick should link to Langfuse traces and support importing Langfuse traces where possible through OpenTelemetry.
