# NeurIPS 2026 Evaluations & Datasets Track

- **URL**: https://blog.neurips.cc/2026/03/23/introducing-the-evaluations-datasets-track-at-neurips-2026/

## Key points

- Explicitly reframed evaluation as a scientific object of study
- Evaluation design, assumptions, tools, metrics, benchmarks, auditing, red-teaming, and documentation shape what claims are justified

## Relevance to Scidekick

Referenced in the science surface. This framing — evaluation as a research object, not just a command output — is a core principle of Scidekick's eval surface.
